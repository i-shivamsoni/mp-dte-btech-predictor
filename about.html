JEE rank & 12th %

This is an independent, open tool to help Madhya Pradesh B.Tech aspirants understand their MP-DTE counselling options. It is built entirely from official public data: the DTE opening/closing-rank cut-off lists (2017–2025) and the 2026–27 intake (seat-matrix) published on the DTE MP Online portal.

It is a static site — all computation happens in your browser, nothing is uploaded, and no rank or percentage you enter ever leaves your device.

Data coverage

The qualifying-exam (12th-%) merit lists are only published for some years, so the percentage predictor currently covers 2019, 2022-23 and 2024-25. The remaining years are being sourced and will be added once available; the JEE-rank predictor already covers 2017–2025.

Disclaimer

Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Directorate of Technical Education, M.P. Predictions are estimates derived from past cut-offs and may differ from future rounds. Always confirm seats, eligibility, and fees on the official DTE portal before making decisions.
