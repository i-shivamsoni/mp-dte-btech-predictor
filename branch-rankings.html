--- page_id: branchrank title: Top colleges by branch subtitle: For each branch, colleges ranked by historical demand — the priority order to fill your choices in. permalink: /branch-rankings/ ---

Demand = how competitive a college’s open/general seat is for that branch — the midpoint of its admitted-rank range (opening–closing); lower = more sought-after. Rankings are per branch — a college can top one branch and sit mid-pack in another — computed purely from the official 2023–25 cut-offs (no reputation adjustment).
