--- page_id: branchrank title: Top colleges by branch subtitle: For each branch, colleges ranked by historical demand — the priority order to fill your choices in. permalink: /branch-rankings/ ---

Demand = how competitive a college’s open/general seat is — lower rank = more sought-after. Ranked per branch from the official 2023–25 cut-offs (no reputation adjustment).

What do Opening / Midpoint / Closing mean? Rank by the opening rank (best/first admitted — surfaces a college with a strong top but a wide spread), the midpoint of the admitted range (default), or the closing rank (last admitted). Each row shows all three so you can see the full spread.
