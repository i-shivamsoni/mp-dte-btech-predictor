--- page_id: branchrank title: Top colleges by branch subtitle: For each branch, colleges ranked by historical demand — the priority order to fill your choices in. permalink: /branch-rankings/ ---

Demand = how competitive a college’s open/general seat is for that branch; lower = more sought-after. Use Rank by to measure it by the midpoint of the admitted-rank range (default), the opening rank (best/first admitted — surfaces colleges with a strong top but a wide spread), or the closing rank (last admitted). Rankings are per branch — a college can top one branch and sit mid-pack in another — computed purely from the official 2023–25 cut-offs (no reputation adjustment).
