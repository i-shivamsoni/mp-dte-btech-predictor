--- page_id: branchrank title: Top colleges by branch subtitle: For each branch, colleges ranked by historical demand — the priority order to fill your choices in. permalink: /branch-rankings/ ---

Demand = how competitive a college’s open/general seat is for that branch (lower closing rank = more sought-after). Rankings are per branch — a college can top one branch and sit mid-pack in another — and are computed purely from the official 2021–25 closing ranks (no reputation adjustment).
