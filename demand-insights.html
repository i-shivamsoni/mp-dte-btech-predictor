Demand trend (2017–2025)

Each branch’s share of all B.Tech seats filled that year — a rising line means students increasingly choose it (share is used, not raw counts, because fewer rounds were published some years). CSE stays on top and climbs (~35% → ~44%), and with its newer AI/ML & Data-Science specialisations the whole CS family now takes ~63% of all seats; Mechanical and Civil fall.
