How MP-DTE admission works — pick your track

MP-DTE fills B.Tech seats round by round. The JEE-Main rounds go first — First Round → First-Round Upgrade → Second Round, allotted on your JEE Common Rank. Whatever seats remain then open in the Qualifying Exam Based Round, allotted on your Class-XII percentage. Anything still vacant after that is filled in College Level Counselling (CLC) — institute-level admission taken directly at the college. Use the predictor for whichever of the first two rounds you’re eligible for.
