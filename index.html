Two admission tracks — pick yours

MP-DTE runs two parallel B.Tech admission channels. The JEE-Main track (First Round → Upgrade → Second Round) ranks you on your JEE Common Rank. The separate Qualifying Exam Based Round ranks you on your 12th-class percentage. Use the predictor for whichever track you’re eligible for.
