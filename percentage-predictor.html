Limited years: the percentage route currently covers 2019, 2022-23 and 2024-25 (the years we hold the qualifying-exam merit lists for). The remaining years are not yet available and will be added later. Your estimate uses the closest available year.
What this round can get you: the Qualifying-Exam (percentage) round runs after the JEE rounds, on whatever seats are left. The most in-demand colleges (SGSITS, IET-DAVV, JEC…) fill up in Round 1 / Upgrade, so they normally have no seats here — this route mainly opens up the mid- and lower-demand colleges that still had vacancies. (See “Seats last to” on Branch ranks for which colleges reach this round.) Anything still vacant after this round is filled in College Level Counselling (CLC) — institute-level admission taken directly at the college (not covered by this tool).

Note: the Qualifying Exam Based Round uses general + EWS + fee-waiver pools only (no OBC/SC/ST reservation in this round).
